About Us

Student-organized, volunteer-run, and completely free.

Our Mission

To inspire the youth about science

We are a student-led nonprofit based in Minnesota on a mission to make science more accessible to students entering high school. Too many studentes get funneled into narrow paths, shaped by which classes happened to be offered, which teacher made an impression, or which opportunities existed at their school, before they've had the chance to discover what genuinely excites them. We hope to offer our experience in a variety of science fields to give students a rich exposure to the breadth of STEM.

Our instructors are from high schools across the Twin Cities metro, many of whom have taken a variety of advanced science courses, competed in olympiads & scientific research, Science Bowl, Science Olympiad, and more.
