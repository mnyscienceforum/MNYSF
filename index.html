What is MNYSA?

Five days of immersive science,
one day of competition.

Minnesota Youth Science Forum is a free, student-organized program designed to prepare middle and high school students for science competitions — and to spark a lifelong love of scientific inquiry. Over five days, students attend intensive classes in Physics, Chemistry, Biology, and Earth & Space Science, taught by top high school students and guided by structured problem sets. The week culminates in a Science Bowl–style tournament on the final day.
