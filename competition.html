Science Bowl Competition

A buzzer-based team tournament covering Physics, Chemistry, Biology, Earth & Space Science, and more.

Format

How it works

Teams of 4 compete in a bracket-style Science Bowl tournament. Matches are buzzer-based, with toss-up and bonus questions spanning all five science disciplines. The morning session features preliminary rounds used to seed and balance teams, followed by single-elimination brackets in the afternoon.

FormatScience Bowl (buzzer-based)
Team Size4 per team
Expected Teams~20
Buzzer Sets8
