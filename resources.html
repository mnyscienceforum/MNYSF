Opportunities

Science Research Summer Programs

Competitive summer research programs for high school students passionate about science.

Research Science Institute (RSI)

Prestigious 6-week program at MIT for high schoolers to conduct original scientific research alongside leading professors.

Summer Science Program (SSP)

Intensive 39-day residential program in astrophysics or biochemistry at top university campuses nationwide.

Simons Summer Research Program

7-week program at Stony Brook University pairing high school students with faculty mentors across STEM disciplines.

Garcia Research Scholars

7-week summer program at Stony Brook University focused on hands-on polymer and materials science research.

BU RISE

Paid 6-week research internship at Boston University placing high school students in active science and engineering labs.
